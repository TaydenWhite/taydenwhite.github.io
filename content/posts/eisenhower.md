One tool I recently learned about is the Eisenhower Matrix. The Eisenhower Matrix is a method of applying weights to tasks based on two metrics, their urgency and their importance.

![Alt text](/posts/matrix.png)

Each of the four quadrants indicates how one should approach a problem based on its varying urgence and importance. It is a very simple process, but one that can greatly simplify a prioritization process.
